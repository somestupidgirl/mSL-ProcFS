ProcFS for macOS

This installs a /proc filesystem for macOS — a kernel extension that exposes running processes and threads as files, with BSD- and Linux-compatible per-process information.

The installer places:

  • the procfs kernel extension in /Library/Extensions
  • the procfs.fs mount bundle in /Library/Filesystems
  • the procfsd daemon and procfs_ksyms helper in /usr/local/sbin
  • the LaunchDaemon in /Library/LaunchDaemons
  • the ProcFS menu-bar app in /Applications

A kernel extension is being installed. After installation you will need to approve it in System Settings and restart — see the final screen for the exact steps.
