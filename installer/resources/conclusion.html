Almost done — two steps remain

Because ProcFS installs a kernel extension, macOS requires you to approve it and restart before /proc can be mounted:

  1. Open System Settings → Privacy & Security, scroll to the security section, and Allow the extension from “Sunneva N. Mariu” / beako. You may need to reboot once for the prompt to appear.
  2. Restart your Mac. On the next boot the mount point is created, the extension loads, and the procfsd daemon mounts /proc for all users.

After rebooting, verify with ls /proc and click the ProcFS menu-bar icon to mount/unmount, toggle Linux mode, or control the daemon.

Auto-load and auto-mount were armed by this installer (/var/db/procfs.enabled). To disable them, remove that file and reboot.
